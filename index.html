About Me

Fullstack Developer

As a software developer and project manager, my career has been a journey of growth and learning. I started out as a junior developer, eager to write code and build solutions that solve real-world problems. Over time, I honed my skills in various programming languages and gained valuable experience in designing and developing complex software systems.
As I progressed in my career, I realized that my passion lies not just in coding, but also in managing projects and leading teams. I took on roles that allowed me to oversee entire software development lifecycles, from requirements gathering to deployment and maintenance. In doing so, I developed a keen eye for identifying potential roadblocks and finding ways to overcome them, while keeping stakeholders informed and engaged. I am a natural problem solver, and I thrive in fast-paced and dynamic environments. I am able to think creatively and outside the box to come up with innovative solutions to complex challenges.
